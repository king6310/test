Nature doesn’t design to match.
Why should we?

Biomimicry is the practice of modeling nature to solve problems through smart design. Our i2® styles do just that, mimicking the random beauty of a forest floor with tile to tile variations in pattern, colour, and texture. Rather than make each carpet tile the same, i2 products vary in patterning and colouring within one style and colourway. The result? Tiles that blend together no matter when they are purchased or installed.

The Benefits of i2

Because i2 styles are designed to blend together, there’s no need to worry about matching dye lots or having a lot of attic stock on hand for selective replacement. And, many i2 carpet tile products are designed to be installed in any order and orientation, which means less installation time and nearly 90% less waste than traditional broadloom carpet. This not only saves time and money, but also the environment. Now that’s genius.
